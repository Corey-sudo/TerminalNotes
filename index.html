Privacy Policy for Terminal Notes

Effective Date: April 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Terminal Notes (“we,” “us,” or “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our iOS app, Terminal Notes.

2. Information Collection and Use

No Personal Data Collected

We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information or usage data from your device. All notes, reminders, and settings remain strictly on your device.

No Analytics or Tracking

We do not integrate analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or any third-party services that collect usage data.

3. Local Storage of Notes

All notes and reminders you create are stored locally on your iPhone or iPad. There is no cloud backup, server synchronization, or remote database. You are solely responsible for any device-level backups (e.g., via iCloud or iTunes).

4. Third-Party Services

Terminal Notes does not use any third-party libraries or services that transmit your data off-device.

5. Security

We follow standard industry practices to safeguard data on the device, but because all information is stored locally, please take appropriate measures to secure your device (e.g., passcode or biometric lock).

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in legal requirements or our practices. If we make material changes, we will revise the “Effective Date” and notify you via the app’s update notes or in-app notification.
